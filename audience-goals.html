Overarching Goals

Client Audience Website
To empower women and children in the fight against human trafficking. To gather information about Rahab’s Rope (who they are, what they do, and what they sell) To make information clear, organized, and easy to find
To create a safe, loving environment in order to distribute medical aid, basic education, and vocational training to both survivors and to those still being trafficked in India. To purchase items from their shop, or make monetary donations to the organization. To raise money and resources through product purchases and donations
To sell “good money” products made by these victims. To become a partner in wholesale distribution in order to offer Rahab’s Rope products in their retail shops. To connect and partner with other small businesses/organizations
To raise funds and awareness about who they are/what they sell. To find opportunities to volunteer or work in India To gain volunteers or new workers wanting to travel to India
To sell other small business goods in stores to help end the circle of poverty in India.
