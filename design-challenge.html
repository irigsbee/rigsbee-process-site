Design Challenge

Wk 5: M2: Part 2b: Assignment: Design Challenge

Framing the Design Challenge:

What is the problem you're trying to solve?

To empower women and children in the fight against human trafficking.

1. The Design Question

How can the website raise awareness and funding in order to provide alternatives to sex work for women and children in India?

2. The Ultimate Impact

To raise funds and support in providing women and children the necessary skills and resources to find freedom from sex work and trafficking.

3. Possible Solutions

Call to action, clearly stated goals, tell the stories of the women and children (personalized), organized site structure, and clarified store/shop locations.

4. Context and Constraints

small organization, only two physical stores in GA, and outreach is aimed towards women in India (I am in neither of those locations). Wholesale store locations are not currently accessible.

5. The Design Question (restated)

How can I clarify and reorganize the Rahab's Rope website to promote funding and support for women and children in the fight against sex trafficking?
