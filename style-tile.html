Style Tile Design

My style tile for the Rahab's Rope website is meant to visualize the key words "warm, inviting, comforting, personal, and empowering". I based the color palette on the colors that Rahab's Rope already had in place (lavender and a dark brown), but with the addition of variations for these colors. The left side of the style tile is meant to give an idea of how the homepage of the website would appear, with the primary CTA underneath a header photo, along with a reorganized menu bar.

I also redesigned the Rahab's Rope logo, from an overly complicated design to a simpler look. Instead of using a mandala flower and rope like they had used before, I chose to use a lotus flower, which is not only significant in Indian culture as a symbol of purity, but it is a symbol of rebirth and regeneration, which I believe is an important representation of the work Rahab's Rope does.
