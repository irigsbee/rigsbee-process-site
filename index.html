Primary Research

Primary Client Research: Updated 2/11/22

My first encounter with Rahab's Rope was at their physical store location in Gainesville, GA. I did not know what the store or organization was until I saw the sign for the small shop on a main street in Gainesville, and knowing Rahab from the Bible I was intruiged to go inside. Upon entering the store, the variety of colorful baskets, textiles, jewelry, and more create an aesthetic and welcoming shop. By each table of products was a printed card or two explaining that everything in the store is handmade by women and children who were previously victims to sex trafficking in India. Rahab's Rope works to provide food, shelter, and alternative jobs to sex workers in India.

Secondary Research

Secondary Research: Updated 2/15/22

The facts: with approximately 40 million people in slavery, it is the fastest growing crime in the world, at a 152 billion-dollar per year industry. India traffics more children than any other country. Every day in India, about two hundred girls and women are forced into the commercial sex trade. Every year, over four million women and children around the globe are trafficked into prostitution and slave labor.

Interviews

Interviews: Updated 2/26/22

For my first interview, I chose my mother, because I knew she had encountered the Rahab’s Rope organization in the past. Then, I chose to ask someone who had expressed interest in giving/volunteering with organizations like Rahab’s Rope, but had not encountered them before. This way, I got the opportunity to hear from both someone who knew Rahab’s Rope, and someone who didn’t in regards to giving their opinions on the organization as well as the site.
